### Summary

Arden Anderson, age 54, was found dead from a gunshot wound at a rest area in Day County, South Dakota, on June 15, 1986. His death was ruled a homicide associated with robbery. Despite multiple investigations and public appeals, the perpetrator has never been identified.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the **South Dakota DCI Cold Case Unit** at 605-773-3331.
